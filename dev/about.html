About

My name is Darren Wong, I started this company in 1992 in the beautiful Kailua, Oahu. Since then I have been providing excellent yard work to happy clients across the state. My favorite part of this job is helping realize a clients dream, helping to plan, design, and build the yard of their dreams. No job is too big or small.

I was born and raised in Hawaii and I am incredibly lucky that my job allows me to help keep this island beautiful. I live in Kailua and have three amazing kids.

If given the opportunity to work with you I will ensure that I will do my all to make sure your project is a success.
